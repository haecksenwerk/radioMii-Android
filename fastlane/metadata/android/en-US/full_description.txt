radioMii — 美音 · beautiful sound

A modern Android app for browsing and playing internet radio streams, built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose (Material 3).

Station data is sourced from the community-maintained radio-browser.info database, enabling fast and efficient station discovery through server-side filtering.

Features:

• Search &amp; discover — browse thousands of stations from around the world, filtered directly on the server for quick results.

• Favorites — save your preferred stations to a personal list for instant access.

• Genre filtering — define your own genres and use them to filter your favorites, giving you full control over how you organize your radio experience.

• News scheduler — when enabled for a station, the player automatically switches to it every half or full hour for 5 or 10 minutes, then seamlessly returns to whatever you were listening to before.

• Sleep timer — set a timer to stop playback automatically after a chosen duration.

• Clean, modern UI built with Material 3.

Available in English, German, French, and Italian.

Minimum Android version: Android 9 (API 28)
